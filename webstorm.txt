- Inspektor
- Debug, breakpoints
- Refaktorisering
- Autocomplete
- Stavning
- Live mallar

    Ruby, Rails, Go, AngularJS, Karma, SQL, Bash
    Dart, Typescript, CoffeScript, SASS, LESS, HAML, SLIM, Gherkin(Cucumber)

- Dubblettsökning
